About Therapy Council

Our Story

Founded in 2018 with a vision to make quality mental health care accessible to everyone in our community.

Therapy Council began when a group of dedicated mental health professionals recognized a critical gap in accessible, high-quality therapy services. We saw too many individuals struggling with barriers—whether financial, cultural, or simply the overwhelming process of finding the right therapist.

What started as a small practice with three therapists has grown into a comprehensive mental health center serving hundreds of clients. Throughout our growth, we've remained committed to our founding principles: personalized care, evidence-based treatment, and creating a safe, inclusive environment where healing can happen.

Today, we're proud to offer a diverse range of therapeutic services, from individual and couples therapy to specialized programs for trauma recovery and family healing. Our team continues to evolve, bringing new expertise and perspectives while maintaining the compassionate approach that defines who we are.

Our Therapeutic Approach

We take an integrative approach to therapy, drawing from multiple evidence-based methods to address each client's unique needs.

Our therapists are trained in a variety of modalities, allowing us to customize treatment plans that work best for your specific situation. We believe that effective therapy combines proven techniques with a strong therapeutic relationship.

Our Treatment Modalities Include:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): Identifies and changes thought patterns that negatively influence behavior and emotions
  • Mindfulness-Based Approaches: Cultivates present-moment awareness to reduce stress and enhance well-being
  • Emotion-Focused Therapy: Works with emotions as a primary source of meaning and direction for positive change
  • Solution-Focused Brief Therapy: Focuses on solutions rather than problems by identifying resources and future hopes
  • Psychodynamic Therapy: Explores unconscious processes and past experiences that influence current behavior
  • Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT): Helps clients accept difficult thoughts and feelings while committing to behavior change

Throughout the therapy process, we regularly check in about what's working and adjust our methods as needed to ensure you're progressing toward your goals. Our aim is to help you not only resolve immediate concerns but also develop insights and skills that will serve you well beyond therapy.
