What happens after my first session?

Your therapist will share a brief summary and a suggested plan based on your goals. You decide the pace—weekly, bi‑weekly, or as needed. There’s no obligation; many clients continue only if they feel a clear benefit.

Are there any hidden fees?

Never. Complete transparency is our promise. The investment you see is exactly what you pay - no surprises, no hidden costs. We believe trust is the foundation of transformation.

Do you accept insurance?

Currently, we don't directly accept insurance, but we can provide receipts for reimbursement if your insurance covers mental health services.

Can I cancel or reschedule sessions?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your session without any charges. We understand life can be unpredictable.

What if I'm not satisfied with my session?

Your success is our priority. If your first session doesn't meet expectations, we'll either match you with a more suitable therapist or provide a complete refund. Your investment is protected.

How do monthly packages work?

Your Transformation Package includes 4 intensive sessions within the month, delivering accelerated results with VIP priority scheduling. Each session builds momentum - unused sessions can be rescheduled within your package month to maximize your investment.
