Our Therapists

Our diverse team brings together expertise in various therapeutic approaches to provide comprehensive care.

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Clinical Director, Licensed Psychologist

Dr. Johnson specializes in anxiety disorders, trauma recovery, and relationship issues. With over 15 years of experience, she integrates cognitive-behavioral, mindfulness-based, and psychodynamic approaches to create personalized treatment plans.

Michael Chen, LMFT

Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist

Michael specializes in couples therapy and family dynamics. Trained in the Gottman Method and Emotionally Focused Therapy, he helps partners and families strengthen their connections and navigate challenges with compassion and practical strategies.

Amara Wilson, LCSW

Licensed Clinical Social Worker

Amara specializes in depression, life transitions, and cultural identity issues. She incorporates solution-focused therapy, narrative approaches, and mindfulness practices to help clients navigate challenges and build on their inherent strengths.

Dr. James Peterson

Licensed Psychologist

Dr. Peterson specializes in anxiety disorders, men's issues, and career challenges. His approach combines cognitive-behavioral techniques with acceptance and commitment therapy to help clients develop practical skills for managing difficult thoughts and emotions.

Elena Rodriguez, LMHC

Licensed Mental Health Counselor

Elena specializes in trauma recovery, grief counseling, and working with the LGBTQ+ community. She uses a trauma-informed, strengths-based approach that incorporates EMDR and somatic experiencing to help clients process difficult experiences and build resilience.

David Okonkwo, LCSW

Licensed Clinical Social Worker

David specializes in adolescent mental health, family systems, and multicultural issues. His integrative approach draws from cognitive-behavioral, family systems, and narrative therapy to help young people and their families navigate the challenges of adolescence.

Administrative Team

Meet the people who ensure our practice runs smoothly and efficiently.

Jennifer Torres

Practice Manager

Jennifer oversees the day-to-day operations of our practice, ensuring that everything runs smoothly for both clients and therapists. With a background in healthcare administration, she brings organizational expertise and a deep commitment to client care.

Robert Kim

Client Services Coordinator

Robert is often the first point of contact for new clients. He handles scheduling, insurance verification, and helps match clients with the therapist who will best meet their needs. His warm, welcoming approach helps make the first steps of the therapy journey easier.

Sophia Martinez

Billing Specialist

Sophia manages all aspects of billing and insurance claims, working to ensure that financial matters are handled smoothly and transparently. She's also available to help clients understand their insurance benefits and explore payment options.
