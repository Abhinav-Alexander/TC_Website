Comprehensive Therapy Services

We offer a range of evidence-based therapeutic approaches tailored to meet your unique needs.

Individual Therapy

Individual therapy provides a confidential, supportive space where you can work one-on-one with a therapist to address personal challenges, develop coping strategies, and work toward your unique goals. Our tailored approach recognizes that each person's journey is different, requiring personalized care and attention.

How Individual Therapy Can Help

  • Develop effective coping strategies for stress, anxiety, and depression
  • Work through past trauma and difficult experiences
  • Build self-esteem and strengthen personal identity
  • Improve relationship patterns and communication skills
  • Navigate life transitions and major decisions
  • Address specific mental health concerns
  • Cultivate greater self-awareness and emotional regulation

Our Approach

We use an integrative approach that draws from evidence-based methods such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), mindfulness practices, psychodynamic therapy, and solution-focused techniques. Your therapist will collaborate with you to determine which approaches will be most beneficial for your specific situation and adjust the treatment plan as needed throughout your therapeutic journey.

Sessions typically last 50 minutes and are usually held weekly, though frequency can be adjusted based on your needs and progress. We offer both in-person and telehealth options for individual therapy.

Couples Therapy

Couples therapy provides a structured, supportive environment where partners can improve communication, resolve conflicts, and strengthen their connection. Whether you're facing a specific challenge, preparing for a major life transition, or simply seeking to deepen your relationship, our therapists can help you build a healthier, more satisfying partnership.

How Couples Therapy Can Help

  • Develop more effective communication patterns
  • Resolve recurring conflicts and improve problem-solving skills
  • Rebuild trust and heal from relationship injuries
  • Navigate major life transitions or decisions together
  • Strengthen emotional intimacy and connection
  • Develop healthier patterns of relating to one another
  • Address challenges related to parenting, finances, or extended family
