Our Treatment Approaches

We use evidence-based therapeutic modalities tailored to your specific needs and goals.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Focuses on identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors that contribute to emotional distress.

Mindfulness-Based Therapy

Incorporates mindfulness and meditation techniques to help manage stress, anxiety, and emotional regulation.

Emotion-Focused Therapy (EFT)

Helps clients access and process emotions in a healthy way to create positive change and deeper connections.

Solution-Focused Brief Therapy

Concentrates on solutions and future goals rather than dwelling on problems and past difficulties.

EMDR Therapy

Specialized treatment for trauma that helps the brain process and integrate traumatic memories.

Acceptance and Commitment Therapy

Helps clients accept difficult thoughts and feelings while committing to actions aligned with their values.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if therapy is right for me?

Therapy can be beneficial for anyone looking to improve their mental health, work through challenges, or simply gain better self-understanding. If you're experiencing persistent stress, relationship difficulties, life transitions, or just feel like you could use support, therapy might be right for you.

How long does therapy typically take?

The length of therapy varies greatly depending on your goals, the issues you're addressing, and your personal progress. Some clients see improvement in just a few sessions, while others benefit from longer-term work. We'll regularly check in about your progress and adjust as needed.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Our billing team can help verify your benefits and explain your coverage. We also offer sliding scale fees and payment plans to make therapy accessible.

What's the difference between in-person and telehealth sessions?

Both formats provide the same quality of care. In-person sessions offer face-to-face interaction, while telehealth provides convenience and accessibility from your own space. Many clients find telehealth just as effective, and some prefer the comfort of being at home.

How do I choose the right therapist?

We'll help match you with a therapist based on your specific needs, preferences, and goals. Factors include the therapist's specialties, approach, availability, and personality fit. You can always request a different therapist if the initial match isn't quite right.
