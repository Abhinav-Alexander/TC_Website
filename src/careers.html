Why This Matters

Every day, people reach out to us feeling overwhelmed, anxious, or stuck. As an intern, you won't just be observing - you'll be part of a team that helps real people navigate their toughest moments. You'll see firsthand how therapy transforms lives, and you'll learn skills that go far beyond a resume.

Why You'll Love It Here

More than just an internship - it's a place to grow

Real Mentorship

You'll work directly with experienced therapists who take time to explain the "why" behind what we do.

Flexibility That Works

We know you have classes, exams, and a life. Work hours that fit your schedule, not the other way around. We care about your growth, not micromanaging your time.

Skills That Matter

Learn communication, empathy, research, and content creation - skills that'll serve you whether you pursue mental health or any other field.

Work That Matters

Every task you complete helps someone access mental health support. You'll see the impact of your work in real time.

Professional Environment

We're HIPAA-compliant, rated 5 stars on Google, and follow evidence-based practices. You'll learn how a professional mental health organization operates.

A Supportive Team

We practice what we preach. Our team values mental health, work-life balance, and supporting each other. You'll feel genuinely welcomed and valued.

Who We're Looking For

We care more about who you are than what's on your resume

Curious & Empathetic

You genuinely care about people and want to understand what they're going through. You ask good questions and listen to the answers.

Reliable & Proactive

When you say you'll do something, you do it. You don't wait to be told what to do - you see needs and step up to help.

Interested in Mental Health

Maybe you're studying psychology, maybe you're not. Either way, you're drawn to understanding how people think, feel, and heal.

Ready to Learn
